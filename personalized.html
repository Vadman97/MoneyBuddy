Types of Financial Aid

Gift Aid

Gift Aid is financial aid that does not need to be repaid, unlike loans. It can be broken down into two categories, scholarships, and grants. Scholarships are generally merit-based, although some can be need-based. Grants are generally need-based. Both can come from federal, state, or private sources.
