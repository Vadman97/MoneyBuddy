Financial Literacy by Students for Students

MoneyBuddy is a resource for students to learn more about financial literacy. We offer help with the FAFSA with deadlines for colleges in the LA area, as well as budgeting tips for students. We offer a survey that will try to match you with a personalized list of scholarships to offset the financial burden of attending a university.
